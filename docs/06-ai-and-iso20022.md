# AI and ISO 20022

Artificial Intelligence is increasingly supporting ISO 20022 migration and operations.

## AI Use Cases

### Message Mapping

AI assists in transforming legacy formats.

### Test Case Generation

AI creates comprehensive test scenarios.

### Data Validation

AI identifies inconsistencies and anomalies.

### Fraud Detection

Richer payment data improves fraud analytics.

## Benefits

* Reduced Manual Effort
* Faster Migration
* Improved Accuracy
* Better Coverage

## Future Potential

Generative AI could eventually automate large portions of message transformation and testing activities.
